How Income Tax is Calculated in the Philippines

Income tax in the Philippines follows a progressive system under the TRAIN Law. This means higher income levels are taxed at higher rates.

Your taxable income is your annual salary minus exemptions and contributions like SSS, PhilHealth, and Pag-IBIG.

What You Should Know

  • ₱250,000 annual income is tax-free
  • Taxes increase progressively
  • Government contributions reduce taxable income
