How Income Tax is Calculated

The Philippines uses a progressive tax system under the TRAIN Law. This means different portions of your income are taxed at different rates.

Only the amount within each bracket is taxed at that specific rate, not your entire income.

Important Notes

  • ₱250,000 annual income is tax-free
  • Allowances may or may not be taxable
  • SSS, PhilHealth, and Pag-IBIG reduce taxable income
  • This is an estimate only
