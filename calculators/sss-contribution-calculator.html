What is SSS Contribution?

SSS contributions are mandatory payments made to the Social Security System to fund benefits such as retirement, sickness, maternity, disability, and death benefits.

Example Contribution

If your salary is ₱20,000, your estimated total contribution is ₱2,800 per month.

FAQ

Who needs to pay SSS contributions?

Employees, self-employed individuals, voluntary members, and OFWs can contribute to SSS.
